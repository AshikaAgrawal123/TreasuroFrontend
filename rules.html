RULES

1. Game Play: At every level, you will be provided with a clue which will point to a location. Once you crack the clue, you will find a QR code at the location scanning upon which you will get access to a string. This string will lead you to the next level.

2. Duration: The online quest will be live for two days.

3. Submissions: Every level holds 10 points. After each wrong submission, the total points will get deducted by 2.The maximum number of attempts for each level is 5. All the submissions of a level will be recorded in our DB so avoid guess work and play fair.

4. Solutions: Every clue will be pointing towards only one location, which can be online or offline.

5. Appeals: There are no appeals in this game but if you think there is an error, feel free to contact us.

6. Judgements:
- Use the unique registration ID provided to you at the time of registration as user name.
- The society holds the right to disqualify any individual in case of any unethical means.

*All the decisions made by the society are final.
